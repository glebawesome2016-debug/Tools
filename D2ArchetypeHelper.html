About This Tool

Destiny 2 Armor Archetype Helper v1.0

This tool helps you identify which armor archetypes to farm based on your desired stat spread.

How It Works:

  • Use the sliders to set your target stats (0-200)
  • Add fragment bonuses to account for your character's fragments
  • Select a weighting strategy (Balanced or Aggressive)
  • Click "Search Combos" to find the best armor archetypes
  • The tool will recommend which archetypes to farm for each armor piece

Weighting Strategies:

  • Balanced (1.5x): Gives equal priority to all your target stats
  • Aggressive (2x): Heavily prioritizes the stats with the highest targets
